From Brief to Built

Our Process

Fully managed end-to-end delivery

4–8 weeks Step 01 Engineering Plan

Design sketch, BIM 3D/4D/5D modelling, structural analysis, energy & sound calculations, full construction drawings.

2–4 weeks Step 02 Material Ordering

Certified materials sourced and ordered. FSC timber, ISO-certified insulation, CE-marked components.

1–2 weeks Step 03 Panel Manufacturing

CNC production in our Kalnciems factory. Quality-controlled assembly, rain-resistant packaging.

On-site Step 04 Delivery & Assembly

BIM-planned truck loading, transport to site, supervised on-site assembly with full warranty bond.
